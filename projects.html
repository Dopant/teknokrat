TEK CONNECT/ALUMNI NETWORK

TekConnect is an initiative under the auspices of the 2019 KNUST Alumni Global Executives (KAGE) where students get to connect with various Alumni in diverse career fields. The TekConnect platform in several folds;

  • Technokrat App - containing an up-to-date database of Alumni, updates on campus events, Alumni community news and updates from fellow alums.
  • TekToday - Update of the Profile of notable alumni (Year Group, Programme offered in KNUST, Profession and others) every week to inform the entire of KNUST community to enhance student-alumni networking.
  • Find Alumni - Find fellow alumni through a search space provided on the Alumni website.

    • OUR HOME/CAMPUS ENHANCEMENT

    A project to model the university into a modern university with state of the art facilities and facilitate beautiful scenearies that would enhance the physical environment of the university.

  • Campus beauty - Green spaces, floral decor/hedges etc.
  • Networks - Asphalt roads through campus with neatly designed speed rumbs and road signs.
  • Washrooms - Washrooms on campus especially at the faculty area must see a facelift.
  • Painting & Maintenance - Buildings painted every year and renovated often.
  • Safety & Security - Safety and Security posts at vantage points to reduce crime rates on and off campus.

    • GENERAL TEKNOKRAT NEWS

    As a Science and Technology University, innovation is an integral component of the day-to-day activities of students on campus. A plan needs to be set up to provide jobs for students on campus, especially needy ones in the area of entrepreneurial activities. The following innovation projects will aid in achieving a set target under innovation:

  • TEK Prize/Idea Challenge - To bring together students with business/entrepreneurship ideas which seeks to provide jobs and create an impact in the society.
  • SHEPreneur - An initiative to engage women with entrepreneurship skills or ideas to cause a change.
  • Challenge - The Photo Challenge is where student photographers participate in the competition/challenge to provide beautiful pictures of our campus in phases with prizes up for grabs. This in collaboration with Ghana Photography Challenge will not only bring out the talents of student photographers but also showcase beautiful pictures of our campus.

    About Us

    The KNUST Alumni Business Hub is basically a center of all innovations and progressive thinkings and ideas. The Aim is to provide KNUST graduates the workspace, platform, connections and resources to work on their long term dreams and ideas right after school so we can have a corporate world filled with KNUST products. Managers, CEOs, Directors and all other portfolios of ownership and trust should be occupied by our graduates. This will help us steer our nation and continent in the direction we want it to be. We believe that as a center of Science and Technology, we have all it takes to create the needed change.
