About Us

The KNUST Alumni Business Hub is basically a center of all innovations and progressive thinkings and ideas. The Aim is to provide KNUST graduates the workspace, platform, connections and resources to work on their long term dreams and ideas right after school so we can have a corporate world filled with KNUST products. Managers, CEOs, Directors and all other portfolios of ownership and trust should be occupied by our graduates. This will help us steer our nation and continent in the direction we want it to be. We believe that as a center of Science and Technology, we have all it takes to create the needed change.
