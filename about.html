Eunice Fosuwaa Akosua Amoako, the KNUST Alumni business hub aims to provide KNUST graduates the workspace, platform, connections and resources to work on their long term dreams and ideas right after school so we can have a corporate world filled with KNUST products. Managers, CEOs, Directors and all other portfolios of ownership and trust should be occupied by our graduates. This will help us steer our nation and continent in the direction we want it to be.
